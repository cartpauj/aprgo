Comment helpers

Tick the fields you want included, fill in their values, then Apply to comment. aprgo encodes each into the APRS-spec wire format and appends them to your beacon comment. Existing encoded fragments are detected and replaced — round-trip safe. Unchecking a field removes it on Apply.

e.g. 1457 → /A=001457 (zero-padded to 6 digits).

A simpler alternative to PHG: declare your coverage radius directly.

Tone is CTCSS (T100) or DCS (D023). Offset is signed kHz for repeater splits.

Receivers derive an estimated coverage radius from these four digits. APRS101 §17.
